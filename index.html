    Top Beijing Attractions

    Forbidden City

    The world’s largest imperial palace, with 9,999 rooms and over 500 years of history.

    Great Wall of China

    One of the world’s wonders and UNESCO heritage sites, offering hiking, photography, and camping.

    Tiananmen Square

    The world’s largest city square, a symbol of Beijing and China’s history.

    Summer Palace

    China’s most beautiful imperial garden, perfect for strolling by lakes and pavilions.

    Temple of Heaven

    A UNESCO heritage site where emperors once prayed for good harvests.

    Beijing Hutongs & Courtyards

    Walk through narrow alleys, visit traditional homes, and discover Beijing’s living history.

    Modern Landmarks

    Explore Bird’s Nest Stadium, National Aquatics Center, and Beijing’s high-tech lifestyle.

    Natural Beauties

    Jingshan Park, Fragrant Hills, and karst wonders like Stone Flower Cave.

    Beijing Tours FAQs

    How do I plan a trip to Beijing, China?

    You can start with a Beijing travel itinerary that includes top attractions like the Great Wall of China, Forbidden City, and Tiananmen Square. Private and customizable tours are available.
    What is the best way to visit the Great Wall of China from Beijing?

    The most popular option is a Beijing to Great Wall of China tour, with private transfers to sections like Mutianyu, Jinshanling, or Badaling. Hiking and night tours are also available.
    Can I walk on the Great Wall of China?

    Yes! You can join a Great Wall of China hiking tour, from easy walks at Mutianyu to adventurous treks at Jiankou or Jinshanling.
    Are there family-friendly tours in Beijing?

    Yes, our Beijing family tours combine cultural immersion, fun activities, and easy-paced sightseeing.
    What food experiences are available in Beijing?

    A Beijing food tour lets you taste roast duck, hotpot, and traditional snacks in old hutongs.
